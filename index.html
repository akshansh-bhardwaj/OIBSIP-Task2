Here's summary of Christopher McCandless's life:

  • 1968: Christopher Johnson McCandless is born on February 12 in El Segundo, California.
  • 1976: McCandless graduates from W.T. Clarke High School in New York.
  • 1986: He graduates from Emory University in Atlanta, Georgia, with a degree in history and honors.
  • 1987: McCandless donates his entire college savings of $24,000 to Oxfam and disappears from his family's life.
  • 1990: He starts traveling under various aliases, working odd jobs and embracing a minimalist lifestyle.
  • 1991: McCandless ventures into the Alaskan wilderness and discovers an abandoned bus near Denali National Park, which becomes his home for the next few months.
  • 1992: In April, McCandless's decomposed body is found in the abandoned bus by hunters. The cause of death is determined to be starvation.
